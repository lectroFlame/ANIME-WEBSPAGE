Top 10 Best Upcoming Anime Of 2025

Anime has been taking the world by storm time after time. Even those who didn't like anime at all begin to try it out and become addicted. Anime has broken the internet so many times and some specific anime will go on to be remember forever but we must look onto the great anime that we will hit us this year. Here are the top 10 must anticipated anime to be released in 2025.

1. SOLO LEVELING SEASON 2

Solo Leveling is an anime television series based on Chugong's South Korean web novel of the same name. After the broadcast of the first season finale, a second season, titled Solo Leveling: Arise from the Shadow, was announced. The season is produced by A-1 Pictures and directed by Shunsuke Nakashige, with Noboru Kimura has taken the internet by storm espacially the iconic word "ARISE!". Has made the next season the most anticipated anime in 2025 and if the animators make even a minute mistake it will trend because of the number of people hyping it.

2. SAKAMOTO DAYS

Sakamoto Days is a Japanese manga series written and illustrated by Yuto Suzuki. It has been serialized in Shueisha's shōnen manga magazine Weekly Shōnen Jump since November 2020, with its chapters collected in 20 tankōbon volumes as of January 2025. The story revolves around Taro Sakamoto, a retired legendary hitman who has settled into a quiet and mundane life as a family man. However, his peaceful life is disrupted when former enemies and colleagues from his hitman days come seeking revenge. To protect his family and loved ones, Sakamoto must use his exceptional combat skills to face off against a variety of adversaries while trying to maintain his ordinary facade. This is also a netflix anime so lets hope its good because its really anticipated and overrated.

3. DAN DADAN SEASON 2

Season 2 is the second season of the Japanese anime adaptation of the Dandadan manga series by Yukinobu Tatsu. The Dandadan season 2 release date was officially announced on December 19, 2024, promising even more of the series’ signature blend of supernatural chaos and heartwarming friendships. In episode 12, titled “Let’s Go to the Cursed House,” Momo, Okarun, and Jiji embark on a journey to uncover the secrets of a haunted house tied to Jiji’s past. Dandadan is an amazing anime combining action, romance, comedy and supernatural genres and events that make the anime engaging in all kinds of anime watchers. Truly well made.

4. MY HAPPY MARRIAGE SEASON 2

My Happy Marriage has come for another season. In an alternative version of the Taishō era in which spirits and magic are real, but in decline, Miyo Saimori, born without supernatural talent, is forced into an existence of servitude by her abusive stepmother. When Miyo finally comes of marriageable age, though, her hopes of being whisked away to a better life crumble after she discovers her fiancé's identity: Kiyoka Kudou, a commander apparently so cold and cruel that his previous would-be brides all fled within three days of their engagements. With no home to return to, Miyo resigns herself to her fate—and soon finds that her pale and handsome husband-to-be is anything but the monster she expected.

5. DR. STONE SEASON 4

Dr. Stone is an anime television series produced by TMS Entertainment based on the manga series of the same name written by Riichiro Inagaki and illustrated by Boichi. Set 3,700 years after a mysterious light turns every human on the planet into stone, genius boy Senku Ishigami emerges from his petrification into a "Stone World" and seeks to rebuild human civilization from the ground up. The single best sci-fi comedy adventure about the smartest guy in the world trying to bring back humanity thousands of years after a cataclysmic event turned everyone to stone is back for the start of its final adventure.

6. FIRE FORCE SEASON 3!!!

To be honest I am really hyped up for the continue of this anime, it is amazing, has one of the best soundtracks and intros. It follows a youth with pyrokinetic abilities who joins a special force developed to counter pyrokinetic monsters threatening Tokyo, which is the sole remaining city in a world ravaged by a global fire. The anime show's second season wrapped in December 2020 and since Atsushi Ohkubo's original manga ended in 2022, fans are even more excited to see the final adventures of Company 8 brought to life – particularly now we have a release date. April 2025 and the latter portion coming in January 2026. There's no way they can ruin this and even if they do I'm still gonna watch and enjoy it

7. KAIJU NO. 8 SEASON 2

Kaiju No. 8 will return for season two in 2025. The release window was confirmed at the end of a teaser trailer that dropped for Kaiju Day 2024, in early August. Sadly, at this point, we don't have a firmer date or time of year, so you'll just have to check back for news and updates. Since Kaiju No. 8 exploded onto our screens showering our eyes with monster blood and guts galore, we've been hooked. But it's not the glorious visuals alone that sustained us throughout season one. It was the oh-so-lovable, goofy (if not slightly desperate) Kafka Hibino and his relentless attempt to join the Defence Force that kept us going. Nothing could stop him from achieving his dreams of becoming a Kaiju hunter, not even becoming a Kaiju himself. Season one saw Kafka fumble his way to a place on the battlefield while trying to keep his secret. But now that his monster identity is out there for the world to see, it can only mean one thing: an exciting, action-packed season two.

8. MY HERO ACADEMIA SEASON 8

The popular superhero anime is coming to an end, as the final season drops in 2025. For eight seasons, My Hero Academia has explored the question of what makes a hero as it tells the story of Deku Midoriya, a young boy who enrolls in a school for superheroes as he chases his dream to become the number one hero in Japan. My Hero Academia has announced that Season 8 of the anime will be serving as the final season, and now it’s been confirmed that the final season will be hitting in the Fall of 2025. My Hero Academia brought Season 7 to an end earlier this Fall as fans saw Izuku Midoriya and the other heroes continue to struggle in the final battles against the villains. But with many of these fights reaching their respective ends, now only All For One and Tomura Shigaraki remain standing as the final opponents to take down before this long and grueling war can come to an end.

9. FRIEREN: BEYOND JOURNEY'S END

Frieren: Beyond Journey's End The series takes place in a fantasy world and follows Frieren, an elf mage on a journey to the resting place of souls to reunite with her former comrade Himmel, whose Hero Party slew the Demon King.The elven mage Frieren is a former member of a party of adventurers who defeated the Demon King and restored harmony to the world after a ten-year quest. The party also consists of the human hero Himmel, the dwarven warrior Eisen, and the human priest Heiter. Before they part, they observe the Era Meteors, a meteor shower that occurs once every fifty years. Frieren agrees to see the group again and offers them an improved view for the next occurrence of the Era Meteors. Frieren departs and travels the world in pursuit of magical knowledge.

10. CHAINSAW MAN MOVIE: REZE ARC

After months of silence, we finally know that the Chainsaw Man movie is officially releasing in 2025. Even better, there's an exciting update arriving in the next few months. Chainsaw Man follows the story of Denji, an impoverished teenager who makes a contract that fuses his body with that of a dog-like Devil named Pochita, granting him the ability to transform parts of his body into chainsaws.
